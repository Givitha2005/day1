1.Difference between HTTP1.1 vs HTTP2

HTTP1.1:
 -It is a standardized protocol for more than 15 years of extensions
 -It loads single request for every TCP connection

HTTP 2:
 -It is faster and more reliable than HTTP1.1
 -It avoids network delay by using multiplexing.
